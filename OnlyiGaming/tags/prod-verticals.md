# Product Verticals (PROD-001 to PROD-010)

## Single Source of Truth

This file is the canonical reference for all 10 product vertical tags. These tags describe **which gaming vertical** an article relates to.

**Cardinality:** 0-2 per article. No primary designation.

---

### PROD-001: Online Casino
**Slug:** `online-casino`

Online casino gaming including slots, table games, and RNG-based casino content. This vertical covers the broadest segment of iGaming, encompassing everything from classic fruit machines to modern video slots with complex bonus features. It includes both the operator side (casino brands) and supplier side (game studios, platform providers).

**Use when article is about:** Slot games, table games, RNG casino content, casino operators, casino platforms, game providers focused on casino content

**Does NOT apply to:**
- Live casino with real dealers (use PROD-010)
- Sports betting markets (use PROD-002)

---

### PROD-002: Sportsbook
**Slug:** `sportsbook`

Sports betting including pre-match, in-play, and related wagering products. This vertical covers traditional sports betting across all sports, from football and tennis to niche markets. It encompasses bookmakers, odds providers, sports data companies, and the technology platforms that power them.

**Use when article is about:** Sports betting, odds, bookmakers, sports wagering, betting exchanges, sports data, in-play betting

**Does NOT apply to:**
- Esports betting (use PROD-006)
- Horse/greyhound racing (use PROD-008)
- Virtual sports simulations (general technology, no specific PROD tag)

---

### PROD-003: Poker
**Slug:** `poker`

Online poker including cash games, tournaments, and poker networks. Poker is distinct from other casino games due to its player-vs-player nature, requiring liquidity management and anti-collusion measures. The vertical includes poker room operators, software providers, and network operators.

**Use when article is about:** Online poker, poker rooms, poker tournaments, poker networks, cash games, player liquidity

---

### PROD-004: Bingo
**Slug:** `bingo`

Online bingo including 75-ball, 90-ball, and bingo network products. Bingo is distinguished by its social/community focus, with chat functionality and side games being core to the experience. The vertical includes operators, platform providers, and network operators.

**Use when article is about:** Online bingo, bingo operators, bingo software, bingo networks, community gaming

---

### PROD-005: Lottery
**Slug:** `lottery`

Lottery and draw-based gaming including scratch cards and instant wins. This vertical covers state lotteries, private lottery operators, and instant win game providers. It is often subject to different regulatory frameworks than other gambling products due to its association with government-backed schemes.

**Use when article is about:** Lotteries, draw games, instant win, scratch cards, lottery operators, lottery platforms

---

### PROD-006: Esports
**Slug:** `esports`

Esports betting and competitive gaming wagering. This vertical covers betting on professional video game competitions (CS:GO, League of Legends, Dota 2, etc.) and the specialized platforms, data providers, and operators serving this market. It targets a younger demographic with different expectations than traditional sports bettors.

**Use when article is about:** Esports betting, esports wagering, competitive gaming, esports tournaments (from betting perspective)

**Does NOT apply to:**
- Traditional sports betting (use PROD-002)
- Esports as entertainment/media without betting angle (no PROD tag needed)

---

### PROD-007: Crypto Gaming
**Slug:** `crypto-gaming`

Cryptocurrency gaming and blockchain-based gambling. This vertical covers operators accepting crypto payments, provably fair gaming, blockchain-based platforms, and the unique regulatory and technological considerations of crypto gambling. It includes both crypto-native operators and traditional operators adding crypto payment options.

**Use when article is about:** Crypto casinos, Bitcoin gambling, blockchain gaming, provably fair, crypto payment integration in gambling

**Does NOT apply to:**
- Blockchain technology news without gambling connection (use NEWS-043)
- Traditional payment processing (use DIR-058/059)

---

### PROD-008: Racing
**Slug:** `racing`

Horse racing, greyhound racing, and other race-based wagering. Racing has distinct traditions, betting types (pari-mutuel, pool betting, tote), and data requirements. The vertical includes racing operators, data providers, and the technology platforms serving this specialized market.

**Use when article is about:** Horse racing, greyhound betting, racing operators, pari-mutuel, tote betting, form guides

---

**Slug:** `multi-product`

Operators or content covering multiple gaming verticals simultaneously. Use this tag when an article discusses cross-vertical strategy, multi-product platforms, or operators that cannot be classified under a single vertical.

**Use when article is about:** Multi-vertical operators, cross-product strategy, platform convergence, operators spanning casino + sportsbook + poker

### PROD-010: Live Casino
**Slug:** `live-casino`

Live dealer gaming with real dealers streamed in real-time. Live casino is distinct from RNG casino games due to its use of physical equipment, professional dealers, and broadcast technology. The vertical includes studio operators (Evolution, Playtech Live, Pragmatic Play Live), technology providers, and the operators featuring live content.

**Use when article is about:** Live dealer games, live casino studios, live gaming technology, game shows with live hosts

**Does NOT apply to:**
- RNG-based casino games (use PROD-001)
- Virtual sports (no specific PROD tag)
