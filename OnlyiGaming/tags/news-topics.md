# News Topics (NEWS-001 to NEWS-045)

## Single Source of Truth

This file is the canonical reference for all 45 news content topic tags. These tags describe **what an article is about** thematically.

**Cardinality:** 2-4 per article. No primary designation — all NEWS tags carry equal weight.

**Description:** Business partnerships, commercial agreements, and strategic alliances between companies in the iGaming space.
