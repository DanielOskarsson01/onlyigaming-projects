# Content Type Tags (TYPE-001 to TYPE-016)

## Single Source of Truth

This file is the canonical reference for all 16 publication type tags. These tags describe **the format/style** of content.

**Cardinality:** 1 per article. Single-select.

---

### TYPE-001: Breaking News
Time-sensitive news requiring immediate publication. First-to-report stories with urgency.

**Use when:** Story is breaking, time-sensitive, first report of a significant event

---

### TYPE-002: Exclusive
Exclusive stories with original reporting not available elsewhere. Based on proprietary sources or unique access.

**Use when:** Story is exclusive, original sourcing, not reported elsewhere

---

### TYPE-003: Interview
Q&A format content with industry figures. Structured as direct conversation with named individuals.

**Use when:** Content is interview format, Q&A style, direct quotes from conversation

---

### TYPE-004: Opinion
Opinion pieces and commentary expressing viewpoints. Author takes a clear position on a topic.

**Use when:** Content expresses opinion, takes a position, commentary, personal perspective

---

### TYPE-005: Analysis
In-depth analysis examining trends, data, or implications. Goes beyond reporting facts to explain why and what it means.

**Use when:** Content analyzes data, trends, provides deep examination, explains implications

---

### TYPE-006: Feature
Long-form features and in-depth profiles. Narrative storytelling with research and multiple perspectives.

**Use when:** Long-form content, narrative style, deep dives, company/person profiles

---

### TYPE-007: Review
Product or service reviews and evaluations. Structured assessment with conclusions or ratings.

**Use when:** Content reviews a product, service, or platform with evaluative judgment

---

### TYPE-008: Press Release
Company announcements and official press releases. Published as-is or lightly edited from company communications.

**Use when:** Content is a press release, official statement, company announcement verbatim

---

### TYPE-009: Report
Industry reports and research publications. Data-heavy content with methodology and findings.

**Use when:** Content is a formal report, research publication, data study

---

### TYPE-010: Guide
How-to guides and tutorials. Educational content teaching readers how to accomplish something.

**Use when:** Content teaches how to do something, tutorial format, step-by-step instructions

---

### TYPE-011: Case Study
Success stories and case study format content. Structured problem-solution-result narrative about a specific implementation.

**Use when:** Content is case study format, success story, implementation example

---

### TYPE-012: Sponsored
Sponsored or paid content with editorial oversight. Clearly marked as commercial while maintaining editorial standards.

**Use when:** Content is sponsored, advertorial, paid placement (also requires COMM-002/003/004)

---

### TYPE-013: Video
Video content as the primary format. Article wraps or accompanies video material.

**Use when:** Primary content is video format, video interview, video report

---

### TYPE-014: Podcast
Podcast episodes and audio content. Article wraps or accompanies audio material.

**Use when:** Primary content is audio/podcast format, podcast episode recap

---

### TYPE-015: Infographic
Visual data content and infographics. Information presented primarily through graphics and data visualization.

**Use when:** Primary content is infographic, visual data presentation, chart-heavy content

---

### TYPE-016: Editorial
Editor's perspective and editorial stance pieces. Represents the publication's official position on a topic.

**Use when:** Content represents editorial position, editor's view, publication stance

---

## Edge Cases

| Scenario | Correct TYPE | Reasoning |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| News article with opinion paragraph | TYPE-001 or standard news | Primarily news; opinion is secondary |
| Interview with analysis framing | TYPE-003 | Interview format dominates |
| Data report with opinion conclusion | TYPE-009 | Report format; opinion is author's interpretation |
| Video interview | TYPE-013 | Video is the primary format |
| Sponsored analysis piece | TYPE-005 | Analysis format; commercial status handled by COMM tag |
