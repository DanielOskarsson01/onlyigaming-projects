## 5. Data Points Gathered

### Pricing/Costs

- **Curacao license:** €20,000-€32,000 initial, €25,000-€50,000 annual — Source: Legal Pilot, GBO Licensing
- **Malta MGA application fee:** €5,000 non-refundable — Source: CSB Group
- **Malta MGA annual fee:** €15,000-€375,000 (B2C Type 1) — Source: CSB Group
- **Malta MGA Year 1 total:** €220,000 — Source: Legal Pilot synthesis
- **Gibraltar license:** £85,000-£100,000 initial — Source: GBO Licensing
- **UK Gambling Commission:** £2,500-£10,000 application — Source: Multiple sources
- **Compliance officer salary:** €60,000-€150,000/year — Source: Legal Pilot
- **Technical compliance testing:** €15,000-€100,000 — Source: Legal Pilot
- **Curacao LOK B2C fee:** €24,490 + €22,960 supervisory — Source: EE Gaming

### Timeframes

- **Curacao license:** 6-8 weeks quoted, 8-12 weeks reality — Source: Multiple sources
- **Malta MGA:** 3-6 months quoted, 4-8 months reality — Source: Multiple sources
- **Gibraltar:** 3-6 months — Source: GBO Licensing
- **UK Gambling Commission:** 16 weeks quoted, 4-8 months reality — Source: Multiple sources
- **US States (PA, NJ):** 9-18 months — Source: SDLC Corp

### Statistics

- **Illegal gambling market:** $1.7 trillion globally — Source: iGaming Business
- **Online gambling market 2024:** $78.66 billion — Source: Market researchers
- **Mobile gambling traffic:** 71.7% in 2025 — Source: Casino.org
- **Curacao audit rate:** 65% of licensed brands annually — Source: Gamblingtalk
