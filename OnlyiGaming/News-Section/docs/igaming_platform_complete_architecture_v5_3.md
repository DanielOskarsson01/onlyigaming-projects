# **iGaming Platform: Complete Multi-Dimensional Architecture & Tagging System**

## **Content + Directory + Platform Integration System**

*Document Version: 5.3 | Date: December 2025*  
*Complete taxonomy with 81 directory categories, 45 news tags, platform features, and migration guide*

## **Executive Summary**

This platform uses a **4-layer tagging architecture** with **8 integrated platform sections**:

### **Architecture Layers:**
1. **Content Tags** - Applied to news articles (geographic, product, type, directory categories)
2. **Directory Tags** - Applied to company listings (81 business categories organized under 11 parent groups)
3. **Section Tags** - Aggregate views that pull from both systems
4. **Main Categories** - Navigation containers (NOT tags) that filter by tag combinations

### **Platform Sections:**
1. **NEWS** - Filtered views of tagged articles
2. **DIRECTORY** - 81 company categories (where articles and companies connect)
3. **MARKETPLACE** - M&A Hub + Project/Bid Board
4. **CONSULTANTS** - Freelancer/Fiverr-style marketplace
5. **MEDIA** - Podcasts, videos, webinars, courses + calendar
6. **EVENTS** - Conferences, trade shows + calendar
7. **CAREER** - Job board and executive moves
8. **COMMUNITY** - Forums, discussions, reviews

**The Power**: Tag once, display everywhere. A single article or company listing can appear in multiple locations based on its tags.

**Total Tags**: 352+ tags across all categories (81 directory, 45 news, 10 product verticals, 16 publication types, 200+ geographic)
